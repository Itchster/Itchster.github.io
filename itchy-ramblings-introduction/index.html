Itchy Ramblings - Introduction

Itchster (Richard Wilson) July 8, 2026

I'm drawn to "blogging" or "Journaling" but rarely actually take the time to sit down and well do it! I'm looking to change that in two ways. 

1. Try to keep a daily journal

2. If I do produce some sort of written prose that I think is worthy of sharing it will be shared here.

Usually, I would probably share things that I think are relevant to the world at large. You might want to read my ramblings about video games, or coding, or moaning about politics and why <insert politician here> is an absolute arsehole.

You probably don't want to read about why <insert person from my private life> is an absolute arsehole, or my struggles in this bloody heatwave. You might want to read about how I'm fixing up my new flat or the joys or horrors of having to have my own garden for the first time, and to be responsible for it (it was overgrown, looked like I was growing wheat). It depends on how entertaining those journals are.

What I'm saying is that if you have stumbled upon this page, i'll post journal entries I think are worthy of reading; don't expect anything regular or consistent; I am neither of those things. But if you are here, welcome!
